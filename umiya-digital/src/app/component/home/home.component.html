About Us

Based in Patdi, Umiya Digital Studio Shot is a creative insight into wedding photography with an exceptional experience of shooting 400+ weddings since 2013.

We are candid and traditional specialists. We are willing to travel across the globe for wedding shoots.

Umiya Digital Studio Shot delivers about 250 proceeded pictures to their clients. They charge 50% advance while booking. Services offered: Candid Photography, Traditional Photography, Pre Wedding Shoots, Wedding Films Albums, Maternity Shoots, fashion Shoots etc.
